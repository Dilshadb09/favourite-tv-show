A series WORTH watching!

This show is set in the 13th century and is based on the life of Ertuğrul, the father of Osman I, the founder of the Ottoman Empire. The series was filmed in Riva, a village in the Beykoz district of Istanbul, Turkey, and premiered on TRT 1 in Turkey on 10 December 2014

About Dirilis Ertuğrul

Possibly one of the best TV show ever made. Ertuğrul is based on a true story. The director made this epic show with so much detail and emotion. The actors are also impressively talented. This is a definite must watch! Ertuğrul features an absolutely mind blowing storyline and action that would keep you glued to your seat. This is a definite binge worthy series to watch.  Once you have started watching Ertuğrul Ghazi you can't stop yourself from watching next episode. I have binge watched the entire series!
